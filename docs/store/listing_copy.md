## Full Description

GymLog is a strength tracker built for serious lifters. Log unlimited workouts, track 1RM and PRs, build up to 4 routines, and access 800+ exercises with full CSV import and export — all free. Data is stored on your device by default, with optional sync when you sign in.

Upgrade to GymLog Pro for $4.99/month or $29.99/year (14-day free trial) to unlock full analytics history, 1-year and all-time chart ranges, and unlimited routines.

No lock-in — export your data anytime as CSV. Import from Hevy, Strong, or any tracker. We don't hide your history behind a paywall; your full workout log is always free to view. Pro unlocks the analytics lens.
