2. Data Storage & Sync

Your workout data is stored locally on your device. If you are signed in, a compressed copy of your workouts, routines, and preferences is synced to our secure database (Supabase) for cross-device access and backup restoration. If you are signed out, your data remains stored only on your device, and we cannot recover it if you uninstall the app or lose the device.
