Delete your GymLog account

This page permanently deletes your GymLog account and all associated data, even if you no longer have the app installed. This cannot be undone.

What will be permanently deleted

  • Your sign-in account and profile on our servers.
  • Any workout, routine, or preference data synced to the cloud.
  • Local data is removed automatically when you uninstall the app or use “Delete account” inside it.

What stays yours

  • CSV files you exported to your device are your property and are never touched.

We require sign-in so only the account owner can request deletion.
